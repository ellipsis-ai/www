The company

We love simple tools.

Humans love simple tools. The best interface is no interface.

We created Ellipsis because we believe there is a better, simpler way to get your work done.

Ellipsis was founded in August 2016 in Menlo Park, California and Toronto, Ontario, with the goal of creating a new kind of platform.
